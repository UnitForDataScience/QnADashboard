Erratum to: The development of lower respiratory tract microbiome in mice

Erratum
Since publication of their article , the authors reported that a requested proof correction had not been carried out in the final published version. The authors had asked for Figure Numbers 2 and 3 to be swapped, and the published version should reflect this.
The original article has been corrected.
